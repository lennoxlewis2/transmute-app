What is Pranayama?

Pranayama is ancient yogic breathwork that directly controls your life force energy (prana). When sexual energy arises, controlled breathing redirects it up the spine rather than letting it discharge downward.

🔥 Breath of Fire (Kapalabhati)

Best used when urges arise suddenly. Burns sexual energy rapidly and raises vibration.

1Sit upright, spine straight. Place hands on knees, palms up.
2Take a deep inhale through your nose to prepare.
3Begin rapid, forceful exhales through the nose — about 1 per second. The inhale happens passively.
4Do 30 rapid breaths. Feel the heat rise up your spine.
5After 30, take a full deep inhale, hold for 10 seconds, then release slowly.
6Repeat 3 rounds. Notice the urge dissolving into energy.
💡 Do this the moment you feel an urge. The energy transmutes in real time.
🌊 Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril)

Balances left and right hemispheres. Calms overactive sexual thought. Best as a daily morning practice.

1Sit comfortably. Use your right hand — rest index and middle finger on your forehead.
2Close your right nostril with your thumb. Inhale slowly through the left for 4 counts.
3Close both nostrils. Hold for 4 counts.
4Release right nostril, exhale slowly for 8 counts.
5Inhale through right nostril for 4 counts. Hold. Exhale through left for 8 counts.
6That's one cycle. Do 10 cycles daily for profound mental clarity.
💡 Practice every morning to keep energy channelled upward throughout the day.
⬆️ Mula Bandha (Root Lock)

Physically redirects sexual energy up the spine. The secret weapon of SR practitioners.

1Sit or stand. Contract the perineum muscles (same as stopping urination mid-flow).
2Hold the contraction firmly. Breathe normally.
3Visualise energy rising up from the base of your spine toward your crown.
4Hold 10–30 seconds, release, repeat 10 times.
💡 Combine with Breath of Fire for maximum effect during peak urge moments.
Guided Transmutation Meditation

This guided meditation uses visualisation and breath to move sexual energy up through your chakra system and convert it into creative and spiritual power. Do this in a quiet space, eyes closed, 10–20 minutes.

🧘
Sexual Transmutation Meditation
~15 minutes · Find a quiet space
Meditation Script
Phase 1 — Grounding (2 min)

Close your eyes. Sit with your spine straight. Take three deep breaths — inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 8. Feel your body settle. You are safe. You are still.

Phase 2 — Acknowledge the Energy (2 min)

Bring awareness to your sacral centre — just below your navel. Notice any warmth or tension without judgement. This is raw life force. It is power waiting to be directed.

Phase 3 — The Upward Draw (5 min)

On each inhale, visualise a golden light rising from the base of your spine. With each breath it climbs higher — heart, throat, third eye. On exhale, let it radiate from your crown as pure white light.

Phase 4 — Transmutation (4 min)

Set an intention. What will you create with this energy? Hold that image at your third eye. Pour the rising energy directly into it. Feel it charge with life.

Phase 5 — Integration (2 min)

Let the visualisation fade. Rest in stillness. Feel the quiet power in your body. This energy is now yours — stored, not wasted. Three deep breaths. Open your eyes.

Why Poses Help

Specific postures build the energetic foundation that supports sexual transmutation. They strengthen the pelvic floor, increase testosterone, improve posture, and circulate stagnant energy.

🐎 Horse Stance (Zhan Zhuang)
Builds immense lower body strength, grounds excess sexual energy, and develops the iron will that SR requires. Used by Shaolin monks for thousands of years specifically for energy cultivation.
1Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointing slightly outward.
2Bend your knees and lower as if sitting on an invisible horse — thighs roughly parallel to the floor.
3Keep your spine straight, chest open, chin slightly tucked.
4Arms extended forward or held in a circle at chest height.
5Hold for 1–5 minutes. Breathe slowly and deeply. Do not let the mind wander.
Start: 1 min · Target: 5 min · Advanced: 10+ min
💡 The burning sensation in your legs IS the energy being cultivated. Breathe through it.
🧘 Ashwini Mudra (Horse Gesture)
Directly contracts and strengthens the perineum. Activates Mula Bandha — the root lock that physically prevents sexual energy from draining downward. Ancient yogic secret for SR.
1Sit cross-legged or lie on your back.
2Inhale deeply. Contract the anal sphincter firmly for 2 seconds.
3Release on the exhale. This is one repetition.
4Do 20–50 repetitions. Build to 100 per day over time.
Daily: 20–100 reps · Can be done anywhere, anytime
💡 Non-negotiable for serious practitioners. This is the physical key to energy retention at the source.
🌉 Bridge Pose (Setu Bandha)
Opens the chest and throat chakras, stimulates the thyroid, and directly engages the pelvic floor while opening the energy channel from base to crown.
1Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor hip-width apart.
2Press your feet into the floor and lift your hips toward the ceiling.
3Squeeze your glutes and engage your pelvic floor.
4Hold 30–60 seconds, breathing slowly. Lower and repeat 5 times.
Hold: 30–60 sec · Sets: 5 · Daily
💡 Visualise energy flowing from root to crown while holding this pose.
🏔️ Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
Corrects posture — one of the most powerful signals of masculine energy. Slouching compresses energy centres. Standing tall opens them. This trains your nervous system to hold your power.
1Stand with feet together, weight evenly distributed.
2Engage your thighs, tuck the tailbone slightly, lift your chest.
3Imagine a golden thread pulling the crown of your head toward the sky.
4Breathe deeply for 2 minutes. Feel the full column of energy from feet to crown.
Hold: 2–5 minutes · Use as a reset throughout the day
💡 Do this first thing every morning before checking your phone. Set the energetic tone for the day.

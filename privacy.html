Privacy Policy

Transmute · Last updated: 21 June 2026

1. Overview

Transmute ("the App") is operated by Lewis Curtis ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App was built with privacy as a core principle.

2. Data We Collect

Transmute does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you enter into the App, including your streak, check-in logs, journal entries, moods, triggers, and weekly reviews, is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

3. Bug Reports

If you choose to submit a bug report through the App, the following information is sent via EmailJS to our developer email address:

  • Your written description of the bug
  • Your current streak day count
  • Your selected colour theme
  • The number of check-in entries you have logged

No identifying personal information (name, email, device ID, location) is included in bug reports. Submitting a bug report is entirely voluntary.

4. Third Party Services

EmailJS: Used solely to send bug reports when you voluntarily submit one. EmailJS Privacy Policy

Google Fonts: Used to load the Inter font. Google may collect standard web request data. Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Billing: If you purchase the Urge Wave feature through the Google Play Store, your payment is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details. Google's handling of payment data is governed by the Google Payments Privacy Notice.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your App data is stored in your device's local storage. It is not encrypted by the App itself, but is protected by your device's own security. We have no access to this data.

If you clear your browser or app data, all stored App data will be permanently deleted. We recommend using the CSV export feature regularly to back up your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Transmute is intended for users aged 17 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 17.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
